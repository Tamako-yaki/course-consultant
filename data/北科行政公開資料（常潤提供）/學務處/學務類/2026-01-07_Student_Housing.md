# Taipei Tech Student Housing Guide

# Student Housing

Taipei Tech Dormitories include different residence halls that are situated on-campus and off-campus. The average traveling time from Taipei Tech off-campus dormitory to the campus is approximately 25-35 minutes via Taipei Metro.

New students are eligible to reside at Taipei Tech Dormitories for the first 2 years.

Residence spaces at Taipei Tech Dormitories from the 3rd year onwards will be decided by lottery and not guaranteed to all. Students entering their 3rd year of study should be prepared to reside elsewhere. Please note that dorm spaces are not guaranteed for Spring Intake students.

## Allocation Details

- **First Year**: All foreign students who have applied for Taipei Tech Dormitories will be allocated to the Taipei Tech On-campus Dormitory.

- **Second Year**:
  - Male foreign students will be allocated to the residence hall at the Xinbei (Haishan) off-campus Dormitory.
  - Female foreign students will be allocated to Nangang Dormitory.

### Taipei Tech On-Campus Dormitory (East Dorm)
- **Address**: No.81, Sec 1, Jianguo S. Rd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City 106, Taiwan
- **Room Type**: 4-person rooms
- **Cost**: NTD 9,900 per semester, plus NTD 1,000 deposit (refundable upon check-out).
- **Discount**: 50% for Overseas Chinese students.
- **Extra Fees**: Charged for air-conditioners and laundry machines.

### Taipei Tech Nangang Off-Campus Dormitory (Females Only)
- **Address**: 5F., NTUT Dormitory, No. 29 Xingzhong Rd., Nangang Dist., Taipei City 115, Taiwan
- **Room Type**: 4-person rooms
- **Cost**: NTD 9,000 per semester, plus NTD 1,000 deposit (refundable upon check-out).
- **Discount**: 50% for Overseas Chinese students.
- **Extra Fees**: Charged for air-conditioners and laundry machines.

### Taipei Tech Xinbei (Haishan) Off-Campus Dormitory (Males Only)
- **Address**: No.241, Sec. 1, Xuefu Rd., Tucheng Dist., New Taipei City 236, Taiwan
- **Room Type**: 4-person rooms
- **Cost**: NTD 7,850 per semester, plus NTD 1,000 deposit (refundable upon check-out).
- **Discount**: 50% for Overseas Chinese students.
- **Extra Fees**: Charged for air-conditioners and laundry machines.
